# 3DSineGame
 To replicate a pulsating 3D structure, with the camera giving a top down view from above with confetti falling downwards. 
The position of your mouse cursor will also affect the **colour** and **brightness** of the structure.

=============================================================================
### Colour - MouseX  
Positioning your mouse cursor towards the **left** will make the structure to be **red** in colour:  

<img width="807" alt="3dsinegameredleft" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/88428142/196119201-69991b0a-9c21-4139-a9c2-216adf300eaf.png">

Positioning your mouse cursor towards the **right** will make the structure to be **purple** in colour:  

<img width="807" alt="3dsinegamepurpleright" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/88428142/196119245-a022e5c8-00cd-4b78-a1e1-965b0538da56.png">

==============================================================================

### Brightness - MouseY  
Positioning your mouse cursor towards the **bottom** of the canvas will make the structure to be **dimmer**: 

<img width="807" alt="3dsinegameupdim" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/88428142/196119546-6696c84e-b62d-4dc0-9fae-603f613decbf.png">

Positioning your mouse cursor towards the **top** of the canvas will make the structure to be **brighter**:  

<img width="807" alt="3dsinegamedownbright" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/88428142/196119510-164a92a4-afac-4246-b801-b69eaee143e5.png">

==============================================================================

### Brightness of one part of the structure only - Slider  
Adjusting the slider at the bottom right will also affect the amount of light being shone at only one part of the structure.  
The **lower** the value, that one part of the structure will appear **dimmer.**   
The **higher** the value, that one part of the structure will appear **brighter.**
